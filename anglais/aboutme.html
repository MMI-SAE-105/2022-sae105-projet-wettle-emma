To know more about me

My name is Emma Wettlé and I'm currently in my freshman year in BUT MMI at the IUT of Nord Franche-Comté. You can find me on Instagram and Pinterest.

This website is the result of a school project of my first semester. Our subject is "Notes for later", I considered it as a mix between a time capsule and a journal. I speak about contemporary society and its main themes: technology, youth engagement and self-acceptance in general. Themes that I think represent the new generation.
