Glossary

The Hunger Games

A dystopian sci-fi film saga, based on the trilogy of books of the same name. We follow Katniss Everdeen who must play a national game to survive.

Link to the trailer

Black Lives Matter

Political movement against racism towards black people. It exists since 2013 in the United States.

Link to the official website of the movement

Ready Player One

This is a science fiction film released in 2018. A paradise-like virtual world has been created in response to a deteriorating and chaotic world. The creator hides a treasure in it, the winner will be rewarded with a large amount of money and the creator's company. A competition is then held in the virtual world.

Link to the trailer

Smart City

It's a city that is both ecological and numerical, that responds efficiently to the needs of its residents and to the needs of the environment.

Link to smart cities website

Digital City

In contrast to the smart city, a digital city is based on technology and the use of digital technology by residents and tourists.

Link to an article on digital cities

Solitaire

A novel by Alice Oseman about the experience of Tori Spring in her last year of high school.

Link to the author's website and content warning

Marina

Marina is a Welsh-Greek singer previously known as Marina and the Diamonds. Her 5th album is Ancient Dreams in A Modern Land and the one that the song comes from.
